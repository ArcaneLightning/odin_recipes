Caesar Salad

Description

This classic Caesar salad is a perfect blend of crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, and crunchy croutons, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and Caesar dressing. Toss until the lettuce is evenly coated.
  2. Add the croutons and grated Parmesan cheese. Toss gently to combine.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately, garnished with lemon wedges if desired.
  5. Enjoy your classic Caesar salad!
