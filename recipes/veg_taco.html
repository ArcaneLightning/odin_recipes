Vegetarian Tacos

Description

These vegetarian tacos are packed with flavor and nutrients, making them a perfect meal for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine black beans, corn, red bell pepper, avocado, and red onion.
  2. Add cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  3. Warm taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  4. Spoon the vegetable mixture into the taco shells or tortillas.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.
  6. Enjoy your delicious vegetarian tacos!
